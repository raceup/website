Why support us

Our mission is to exceed our limits every year and to realize, through our creativity, capability and competence, our racing cars in line with the high technological and innovative standards of the automotive world. To make all this possible, we work with the best companies in the industry, sharing with them innovation and expertise.
The Race Up team is one of the most important Formula Student university teams in Italy. Our commitment and expertise gained in 12 years of activity has led us to distinguish ourselves in various European competitions, some of which reach the 5000 participants:

  • Formula Student Germany (Hockenheim)
  • Formula Student Italy (Autodromo Riccardo Paletti)

Every year we also participate in events that bring greater visibility to our partners not only in our territory but also abroad:

  • International fairs (Mec-Spe)
  • Notte dei ricercatori
  • Galileo Festival
  • Sperimentando
  • TEDx
